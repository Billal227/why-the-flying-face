How To Play

🖥️ PC Controls:

Press SPACE to flap/jump

Collect glowing powerups by flying into them

📱 Mobile Controls:

Tap anywhere on screen to flap/jump

Fly into glowing powerups to collect them

🛡️ Powerups:

Blue Shield — protects from obstacles (spikes, fireballs, asteroids) for 6 seconds

Green Bubble — protects from pipes for 4 seconds

🌍 Biomes:

🌿 Valley — 0 to 9 points

🏜️ Desert — 10 to 19 points

🧊 Glacier — 20 to 29 points

🌋 Lava — 30 to 39 points

🚀 Space — 40+ points

💡 Tips:

Difficulty increases each biome

Only one powerup active at a time

Collecting a new powerup replaces the current one
